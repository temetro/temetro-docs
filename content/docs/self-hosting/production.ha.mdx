Turawar production na nufin an amince da tsarin don amfani na yau da kullum. A wannan matakin, dogaro da tsaro suna da muhimmanci kamar app ɗin kansa.

## Jerin dubawa na production

Kafin a fara aiki, tabbatar da waɗannan:

- sabar na da wutar lantarki mai ƙarfi da sanyaya mai kyau
- an saita backup na database kullum ko bisa jadawali
- `APP_URL` yana amfani da ainihin hostname da ma'aikata za su buɗe
- `BETTER_AUTH_SECRET` an saita shi da ƙima mai ƙarfi da aka samar
- an saita reverse proxy da HTTPS
- an takaita damar shiga sabar ga admins da aka amince da su kawai

## Yi amfani da production Compose override

Fara stack da:

```bash
docker compose -f docker-compose.yml -f docker-compose.prod.yml up -d
```

Wannan yana ƙara sabis na NGINX kuma yana kunna halayen da suka dace da production.

## Ayyukan NGINX

A cikin stack na Temetro, NGINX yana da alhakin:

- sauraron tashoshin `80` da `443`
- tura dashboard zuwa web service
- tura `/docs` zuwa docs service
- sarrafa compression da security headers
- samar da sauƙaƙƙen endpoint na `/health`

## HTTPS

A kowane muhalli da bayanan marasa lafiya ko ma'aikata ke wucewa ta hanyar sadarwa, HTTPS ya zama dole.

Za ka iya saka certificates a cikin babban fayil na `ssl` da NGINX ke amfani da shi. Wasu ƙungiyoyi suna amfani da:

- takardun shaida na ciki daga IT na asibiti
- takardun shaida na jama'a daga hukumomin bayarwa
- kayan aikin atomatik da ake sarrafawa a waje da stack na Temetro

## Dabarar backup

Mafi ƙarancin shiri mai kyau ya haɗa da:

- PostgreSQL dumps na dare
- cikakken snapshot na sabar ko VM kowane mako
- kwafi mai tsaro na backup na baya-bayan nan a wajen na'urar
- tsarin dawo da bayanai da aka rubuta kuma aka gwada

## Dabarar sabuntawa

Ga asibitocin da ke da ƙarancin lokacin kulawa:

1. yi backup na database
2. tsara lokacin canji bayan awannin aiki
3. ja sabuwar code
4. sake gina sabis
5. tabbatar da shiga da dashboard na marasa lafiya

## Tsarin tallafi

Saboda Temetro bude-tushe ne, ya kamata asibitoci su yanke shawara tun da wuri wanene zai kula da:

- patching na sabar
- sabunta app
- kula da shaidun shiga
- backup na database
- mayar da martani ga abubuwan da suka faru

Idan waɗannan alhakin sun bayyana, ko ƙananan ƙungiyoyi ma za su iya gudanar da tsarin cikin kwanciyar hankali.
