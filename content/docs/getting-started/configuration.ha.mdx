An tsara Temetro ya kasance mai sauƙi. Yawancin shigarwa suna buƙatar ƙimomin muhalli kaɗan ne kawai don farawa cikin aminci.

## Muhimman ƙimomin muhalli

Babban ƙimomin suna cikin fayil ɗin `.env` na ma'ajiyar turawa.

| Ƙima | Dole | Misali | Aiki |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| `POSTGRES_PASSWORD` | Eh | `strong-password-here` | Kalmar sirrin mai amfani da PostgreSQL |
| `APP_URL` | Eh | `http://localhost:3000` | URL na jama'a don dashboard |
| `BETTER_AUTH_SECRET` | Eh | ƙima da aka samar | Sirrin sa hannu da kariyar auth |
| `SMTP_HOST` | Zaɓi | `smtp.example.org` | Sunan sabar imel |
| `SMTP_PORT` | Zaɓi | `587` | Tashar sabar imel |
| `SMTP_USER` | Zaɓi | `alerts@example.org` | Sunan mai amfani SMTP |
| `SMTP_PASS` | Zaɓi | `mail-password` | Kalmar sirri SMTP |

## Saitunan farko da aka ba da shawara

Don muhalli na gwaji:

- saita `APP_URL=http://localhost:3000`
- yi amfani da kalmar sirri mai ƙarfi ga bayanan database
- samar da sabon `BETTER_AUTH_SECRET`

Don ainihin muhalli na asibiti:

- yi amfani da sunan DNS na ciki ko domain ɗin da ma'aikata za su yi amfani da shi
- ajiye `.env` a wuri mai tsaro tare da iyakantattun izini
- riƙe kwafin `BETTER_AUTH_SECRET` don dawo da bayanai idan matsala ta faru

## Haɗin database

Aikin web yana gina `DATABASE_URL` ta atomatik a cikin Docker ta amfani da ƙimomin Compose. Sau da yawa ba sai ka gyara URL ɗin database da hannu ba.

Idan daga baya ka koma PostgreSQL na waje, sabunta host da takardun shaidar domin dashboard ya iya haɗuwa cikin aminci.

## Shawarar cibiyar sadarwa ga ƙungiyoyin IT na asibiti

Idan asibiti yana da hanyar sadarwa ta cikin gida amma intanet ɗin ta iyakance:

- masauki Temetro a kan na'ura da ke cikin wurin
- ba shi IP mai dindindin ko sunan DNS na ciki
- tabbatar kwamfutocin ma'aikata suna iya isa tashoshin `80` ko `3000`
- yi amfani da HTTPS ta NGINX kafin ka ba da damar shiga daga nesa

## Ajiyar baya

Temetro yana adana bayanai masu muhimmanci a PostgreSQL. Ka sanya ajiyar bayanan database cikin al'adunka tun daga rana ta farko.

Aƙalla, ajiye:

- bayanan PostgreSQL
- fayil ɗin `.env`
- duk wani reverse proxy ko fayilolin SSL
