---
title: Farawa na farko
description: Tabbatar da sabon shigarwar Temetro kuma shirya aikin farko na mai gudanarwa.
---

Wannan shafin yana taimaka maka ka tabbatar Temetro ya fara daidai kuma ƙungiyarka za ta iya amfani da shi cikin aminci.

## 1. Duba lafiyar kontena

Gudanar:

```bash
docker compose ps
```

Dukkan manyan ayyuka ya kamata su nuna healthy ko running.

Idan PostgreSQL har yanzu yana sake farawa, jira kaɗan sannan ka sake gudanar da umarnin.

## 2. Buɗe dashboard

Je zuwa URL na dashboard a browser:

```text
http://localhost:3000
```

Ya kamata ka isa shafin shiga.

## 3. Ƙirƙiri asusun admin na farko

Yi amfani da **Create an account** a shafin shiga don yin rajistar admin na farko. Zaɓi adireshin imel na ƙungiyar aiwatarwa ko shugaban IT.

Shawarar bayanin farko:

- Suna: mutumin da ke da alhakin ƙaddamarwa ko sa ido
- Imel: adireshin hukuma mai ɗorewa
- Kalmar sirri: jimlar kalma mai ƙarfi da aka ajiye a manhajar kalmomin sirri na asibiti

## 4. Duba dashboard na farko

Bayan shiga, tabbatar manyan sassan suna lodawa da kyau:

- Bayanin dashboard
- Marasa lafiya
- Alƙawura
- Ma'aikata
- Saituna

A wannan mataki, wasu bayanai na gwaji ne don tabbatar da shimfiɗa da hanyoyin aiki.

## 5. Seed ko migrate bayanai idan ana buƙata

Idan kana son bayanan gwaji, gudanar da:

```bash
npm run seed
```

Ko cikin Docker:

```bash
docker compose exec web npm run seed
```

## 6. Shirya miƙa aiki

Kafin ma'aikatan asibiti su fara gwaji, tabbatar ka:

- rubuta URL na shiga
- san wanda ke riƙe da takardun admin na farko
- yanke shawarar inda za a ajiye backups
- tabbatar da wanda zai kula da updates da sa ido kan sabar

## Menene na gaba

Idan tsarin yana aiki a gida, ci gaba da sashen masaukin kai domin tsara turawar sabar mai ɗorewa.
