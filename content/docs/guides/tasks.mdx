The **Tasks** page is a shared to-do list for your clinic. A task is either
**personal** (assigned to yourself) or assigned to a **department** — every member of
that department sees it in their list.

## Using the list

### Creating a task

1. Open **Tasks** and click **New task**.
2. Give it a **subject** ("Call Mrs. Hassan with lab results"), and optionally
   free-text **details**.
3. Under **Assignee**, pick a tab:
   - **Myself** — a personal task only you (and admins) see.
   - **Other** — choose a **department**: Admin, Doctor, Reception, **Pharmacy**, or
     **Lab**.
4. Optionally set a **due date** and a **priority** (high / medium / low), then add it.

### Working the list

- Filter by **All / Open / Done** at the top.
- Tick the checkbox to mark a task **done** — done tasks stay in the history rather
  than vanishing. Open a task to see its full details.
- Each task shows which department it's for and who created it, so it's easy to follow
  up with questions.

<Callout type="info" title="Who sees what">
  You see tasks you created plus tasks assigned to your department; owners and admins
  see every task in the clinic. Lab members also get their department's queue on the
  Lab dashboard.
</Callout>

Task changes appear in the [activity feed](/docs/guides/activity-analytics) like every
other change in temetro.
