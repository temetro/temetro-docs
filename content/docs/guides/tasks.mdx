The **Tasks** page is a **kanban board** — a shared to-do list for your clinic, with
cards you drag between columns as work progresses.

## Using the board

### Creating a task

1. Open **Tasks** and click **New task**.
2. Give it a **title** ("Call Mrs. Hassan with lab results"), and optionally:
   - an **assignee** (a member of your clinic) and their role,
   - a **due date**,
   - a **priority**,
   - a linked **patient**,
   - free-text **notes**.
3. Save. The card appears on the board for everyone in the clinic.

### Working the board

- Drag cards between columns, or open a card to update it.
- Mark a task **done** when it's finished — done tasks stay in the history rather than
  vanishing.
- Each card shows who created it, so it's easy to follow up with questions.

Everyone except viewers can create and update tasks; viewers can read the board.
Task changes appear in the [activity feed](/docs/guides/activity-analytics) like every
other change in temetro.
