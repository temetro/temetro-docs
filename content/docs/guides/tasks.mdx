The **Tasks** page is a shared **kanban board** for your clinic. A task can be
**personal** (assigned to yourself), assigned to a **department** — every member of that
department sees it — or assigned to a **specific person**, who then sees it on their board.

## Using the board

The board has three columns — **To Do**, **In Progress**, and **Done** — each showing a
count and an **Add task** button.

### Creating a task

1. Open **Tasks** and click **Add task** (use a column's button to start it there, or
   **New task** at the top).
2. Give it a **subject** ("Call Mrs. Hassan with lab results"), and optionally
   free-text **details**.
3. Under **Assignee**, pick a tab:
   - **Myself** — a personal task only you (and admins) see.
   - **Department** — choose a department: Admin, Doctor, Reception, **Pharmacy**, or
     **Lab**.
   - **Person** — pick a specific clinician from your clinic's providers; the task shows
     up for them by name.
4. Optionally set a **due date**, a **priority** (high / medium / low), and a starting
   **status** (column), then add it.

### Working the board

- **Drag a card** between columns to change its status, or open it and use **Move to**.
  Moving a task to **Done** marks it complete.
- Each card shows its **due date** and **priority**, plus which department it's for and
  who created it — so it's easy to follow up. Click a card for full details.

<Callout type="info" title="Who sees what">
  You see tasks you created, tasks assigned to your department, and tasks assigned to you
  personally; owners and admins see every task in the clinic. Lab members also get their
  department's queue on the
  Lab dashboard, where each item **expands** to show its notes, status, patient,
  assignee and creator alongside a complete button.
</Callout>

Task changes appear in the [activity feed](/docs/guides/activity-analytics) like every
other change in temetro.
