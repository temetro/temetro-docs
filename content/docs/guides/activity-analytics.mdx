## Activity

The **Activity** page is your clinic's audit trail.

### What gets logged

Every change to records — creating a patient, editing medications, booking an
appointment, completing a task — is logged automatically with:

- **who** made the change,
- **what** was changed (the type of record and, where relevant, the patient),
- **when** it happened.

### Who can see it

What you see depends on your role: clinic **admins and owners see all activity**;
everyone else sees their own. Nobody can edit or delete the log — that's the point.

<Callout type="info" title="Why this matters">
  The activity log is today's answer to accountability. The longer-term
  [patient-owned records vision](/docs/concepts#patient-owned-records-the-vision) goes
  further: changes will be cryptographically signed and approved by the patient.
</Callout>

## Analytics

The **Analysis** page is a dashboard of your clinic's aggregates — patient counts,
appointment volume, prescriptions, and task throughput — so you can see at a glance how
the clinic is doing. Every member of the clinic can view it.

At the top, a **Live** panel shows a real-time line chart of patients currently in the
building. Below the headline numbers, trends are drawn as charts — an area chart of
patient growth by month and a bar chart of appointments by weekday.
