The **Notes** page is your personal notebook inside temetro. Notes are **private to
you** — other members of the clinic cannot read them — which makes them right for
drafts, reminders, and personal working notes rather than the official chart.

## Your notebook

### Writing notes

1. Open **Notes** and click **New note**.
2. Give it a title and write. The editor supports **rich text** — headings, bold,
   lists, and so on — so notes can be more than plain text.
3. Notes save to your account and are there whenever you come back, on any device.

### Notes vs. the patient chart

<Callout type="info">
  Anything that belongs in the medical record — encounter summaries, problems,
  medications — should go on the [patient's record](/docs/guides/patients), where it's
  shared with the care team and tracked in the activity log. Notes are for *your own*
  thinking.
</Callout>
