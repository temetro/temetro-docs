**Meetings** are voice/video calls for your staff — a Discord-style set of rooms your team
can hop into. Open **Messages → Meetings** in the sidebar.

## Joining a call

1. Open **Messages → Meetings**.
2. Pick a room from the list to join the call, or click **New room** to create one.
3. Your browser asks for camera and microphone permission the first time — allow it.
4. You'll see a tile for yourself and one for each other person in the room.

## In-call controls

The control bar at the bottom of the room has:

- **Microphone** — mute/unmute yourself.
- **Camera** — turn your video on or off (others see your avatar when it's off).
- **Share screen** — present a screen or window; click again (or "Stop sharing") to
  return to your camera.
- **Leave** — drop out of the call. The room stays for others.

## Good to know

- Rooms hold up to **4 people**. Calls connect people **peer-to-peer**, so audio and
  video never pass through temetro's servers.
- Rooms are shared across the clinic — anyone on your team can create or join one.

<Callout type="info">
  Calls work out of the box on the same network or with simple home/office routers. If your
  clinic is split across networks behind strict firewalls, your administrator may need to
  configure a TURN server for media to connect.
</Callout>
