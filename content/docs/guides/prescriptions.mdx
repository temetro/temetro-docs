The **Prescriptions** page is your clinic's prescription ledger: every medication
prescribed, to whom, by whom, and its current status.

## The prescription ledger

### Writing a prescription

1. Open **Prescriptions** and click **New prescription**.
2. Pick the **patient** — search by name or file number and use the **arrow keys + Enter**
   to choose without leaving the keyboard.
3. Enter the **medication**: as you type, matching items from your
   [inventory](/docs/guides/pharmacy) are suggested (with stock levels). Pick one, or just
   type a name freely when there's no match.
4. Set the **dose** and **frequency** (e.g. "500 mg", "3× daily"), and optionally a
   **duration** and free-text **notes**.
5. *(Optional)* Click **Add start/end dates** to record an explicit course window. When an
   **end date** is set it drives the expiry/"expiring" badge on the pharmacy queue instead
   of the duration text.
6. The **prescriber** defaults to you. Save.

### Tracking

Each prescription has a status (e.g. active, completed, expired) you can update as
treatment progresses. The **search box** at the top of the page filters the ledger by
patient name, file number, medication, prescriber, or status — so pulling up
everything a patient is currently taking is quick.

<Callout type="info" title="Who can prescribe">
  Doctors, members, admins, and owners can create and manage prescriptions.
  **Reception cannot see prescriptions at all** — they are clinical data. **Pharmacy**
  can review and update prescriptions (e.g. mark them completed) but cannot create or
  delete them. See [Roles & permissions](/docs/admin/roles-permissions).
</Callout>

## Pharmacy dashboard

Members with the **Pharmacy** role land on the **Pharmacy** page — a dispensing view
over the same ledger:

- **At a glance:** active prescriptions, courses **expiring soon** (≤2 days left, from the
  end date when set, else the start/prescribed date plus duration), and how many patients
  are currently on medication.
- **Dispensing queue:** active prescriptions, oldest first, with the same search box.
  Courses about to run out carry an **Expiring** badge.
- **Dispense:** one click on a queue row hands over the course — it's marked completed and
  logged to the **Recently dispensed** feed; clicking the row itself opens the full
  prescription detail.

Clinicians can also open the Pharmacy page for an oversight view of the queue.

Prescription changes are recorded in the
[activity log](/docs/guides/activity-analytics), giving you a full history of who
prescribed what and when.
