title: Tabbatarwa
description: Duba saitin Better Auth da dashboard ɗin Temetro ke amfani da shi.
---

Temetro yana amfani da Better Auth a matsayin matakin farko na tabbaci.

## Tsarin tabbaci na yanzu

Scaffold ɗin yana kunna:

- shiga ta imel da kalmar sirri
- tabbaci na session
- hanyoyin dashboard masu kariya
- duba session daga bangaren sabar

## Tsarin route

Dashboard yana fallasa Better Auth a ƙarƙashin:

```text
/api/auth/[...all]
```

Wannan shi ne daidaitaccen tsarin route da ake ba da shawara ga Next.js App Router.

## Muhallin canje-canjen da ake buƙata

- `BETTER_AUTH_SECRET`
- `BETTER_AUTH_URL`
- `NEXT_PUBLIC_APP_URL`
- `DATABASE_URL`

## Tsawon session

Temetro a halin yanzu yana amfani da taga session na kwanaki bakwai. Wannan ya isa ga aikin yau da kullum na ma'aikata yayin da yake kiyaye iyakokin shiga.

## Bayanan tsaro

A production, ka duba waɗannan da kyau:

- samar da sirrin auth na musamman
- kare sabar da HTTPS
- takaita damar shiga database ga hanyoyin sadarwa da aka amince da su
- yanke shawarar wanda zai iya ƙirƙira ko sarrafa asusun admin

## Faɗaɗa na gaba

Tushen Better Auth na iya fadada zuwa:

- tabbatar da imel
- sake saita kalmar sirri
- tabbaci na mataki biyu
- organizations da cikakkun izini

Wadannan damar suna dacewa da stack ɗin yanzu ba tare da sake rubuta dukkan layin auth ba.
