---
title: Xaqiijin
description: Dib u eeg dejinta Better Auth ee dashboard-ka Temetro.
---

Temetro waxa uu adeegsadaa Better Auth sidii lakabka koowaad ee xaqiijinta.

## Qaabka xaqiijinta ee hadda jira

Scaffold-ku waxa uu daarayaa:

- gelitaan email iyo eray-sir
- xaqiijin ku salaysan session
- routes-ka dashboard-ka oo la ilaaliyo
- hubinta session-ka server-ka gudihiisa

## Qaab-dhismeedka route-yada

Dashboard-ku waxa uu Better Auth ku soo bandhigaa:

```text
/api/auth/[...all]
```

Tani waa qaabka route-ka caadiga ah ee lagu taliyay marka la isticmaalayo Next.js App Router.

## Doorsoomayaasha deegaanka ee loo baahan yahay

- `BETTER_AUTH_SECRET`
- `BETTER_AUTH_URL`
- `NEXT_PUBLIC_APP_URL`
- `DATABASE_URL`

## Mudada session-ka

Temetro waxa uu hadda isticmaalaa daaqad session oo toddoba maalmood ah. Tani way ku filan tahay shaqada caadiga ah ee shaqaalaha iyadoo weli gelitaanku xaddidan yahay.

## Qoraallo amni

Marka wax-soo-saar la isticmaalayo, si taxaddar leh u eeg:

- samee sir xaqiijin oo gaar ah
- ku ilaali server-ka HTTPS
- xaddid gelitaanka hifadhidata shabakadaha la aamini karo
- go'aami cidda abuuri karta ama maamuli karta akoonnada admin-ka

## Ballaarin mustaqbalka

Aasaaska Better Auth waxa lagu kordhin karaa:

- xaqiijinta email-ka
- dib-u-dejinta erayga sirta ah
- xaqiijinta laba-tallaabo ah
- ururro iyo rukhsado faahfaahsan

Awoodahani si dabiici ah ayay ugu habboon yihiin stack-ga hadda jira iyada oo aan dib loo qorin lakabka xaqiijinta oo dhan.
