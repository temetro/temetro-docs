title: Tsarin backend
description: Fahimci yadda Next.js, Better Auth, da sabis na Express na ciki suke raba ayyuka yanzu.
---

Yanzu Temetro yana tafiyar da backend na dashboard a matakai biyu maimakon guda ɗaya.

## Rarrabuwar runtime a yanzu

- Next.js yana ba da UI na dashboard
- Better Auth har yanzu yana gudana a cikin Next.js
- sabis na Express na ciki yana ba da bayanan dashboard

Wannan yana sa web app ɗin da mai amfani yake gani ya kasance mai daidaito yayin da ake matsar da kwangilolin bayanan dashboard zuwa sabis na backend na musamman.

## Hanyoyin cikin gida

Sabis ɗin Express a yanzu yana bayar da:

- `GET /health`
- `GET /dashboard/home/:role`

Waɗannan hanyoyin an yi su ne don amfani na cikin gida tsakanin sabis, ba don buɗaɗɗen amfani kai tsaye daga browser ba.

## Muhalli da haɗin sabis

Dashboard na Next.js yana karanta daga sabis na Express ta:

- `INTERNAL_API_URL`

A cikin Docker Compose, ƙimar da aka saba ita ce:

```text
http://api:4000
```

## Dalilin da Temetro ke amfani da wannan rarrabuwa

Wannan matakin yana ba Temetro:

- iyaka mafi tsabta ga kwangilolin bayanan dashboard
- damar faɗaɗa backend ba tare da sake rubuta auth gaba ɗaya ba
- hanya mafi sauƙi don faɗaɗa API a gaba

Auth da routing na shafuka har yanzu suna cikin Next.js a yanzu domin canjin ya kasance cikin kulawa.
