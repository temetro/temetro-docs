The patient workspace is designed to make high-volume clinical operations easier to manage without overwhelming staff.

## What the patient area includes

- A responsive patient list
- Assigned clinician information
- Status and next review details
- A patient detail page for a quick clinical summary

## Intended workflow

Front desk, nursing, and clinical staff can use the same workspace to:

- Register new patients
- Locate a patient quickly
- Confirm who is responsible for care
- Review upcoming visits or discharge plans

## Why it matters

Many hospital teams still depend on fragmented spreadsheets, paper registers, or messaging groups to coordinate patient flow. Temetro brings that information into one consistent interface that is easier to review on desktop and tablet.

## Data model highlights

The current schema includes:

- Unique patient number
- Name and date of birth
- Gender and blood type
- Contact information
- Assigned doctor
- Appointments
- Medical records

This foundation makes it easier to extend Temetro later for triage, billing, laboratory, or pharmacy workflows.
