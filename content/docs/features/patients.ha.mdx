---
title: Marasa lafiya
description: Fahimci yadda Temetro ke tsara bayanan marasa lafiya da hangen aikin asibiti na yau da kullum.
---

An tsara ɓangaren marasa lafiya don sauƙaƙa ayyukan asibiti masu yawa ba tare da nauyaya wa ma'aikata ba.

## Abin da ɓangaren marasa lafiya ya ƙunsa

- jerin marasa lafiya mai amsawa
- bayanin likitan da aka ɗora wa alhaki
- matsayi da ranar bita ta gaba
- shafin cikakken bayani na mara lafiya don saurin taƙaitaccen duba lafiya

## Hanyar aiki da aka nufa

Front desk, jinya, da ma'aikatan asibiti za su iya amfani da wannan wuri don:

- rajistar sabbin marasa lafiya
- nemo mara lafiya cikin sauri
- tabbatar da wanda ke kula da jinyar
- duba ziyarar gaba ko shirin sallama

## Dalilin muhimmancinsa

Yawancin asibitoci har yanzu suna amfani da takardun rubutu, rijistar takarda, ko saƙonnin rukuni wajen daidaita kwararar marasa lafiya. Temetro yana haɗa wannan bayanin a cikin fasali guda ɗaya.

## Muhimman abubuwan tsarin bayanai

Schema na yanzu ya ƙunshi:

- lambar mara lafiya ta musamman
- suna da ranar haihuwa
- jinsi da nau'in jini
- bayanan tuntuɓa
- likitan da aka ɗora wa alhaki
- alƙawura
- bayanan lafiya

Wannan tushe yana sa ya zama sauƙi a faɗaɗa Temetro zuwa triage, billing, laboratory, ko pharmacy nan gaba.
