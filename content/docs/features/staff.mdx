Hospitals do not only need patient records. They also need clarity about who is on shift, where they are assigned, and which unit needs support.

## Staff workspace goals

The staff area is intended to support:

- Role visibility
- Unit assignments
- Shift communication
- Simple staff administration

## Early implementation focus

The scaffold currently emphasizes a clear operational layout rather than a deep HR system. It is designed to be extended for:

- Permissions and role-based access
- Team rosters
- Department leadership workflows
- Multi-facility structures

## Why this matters in practice

In many healthcare settings, staffing adjustments happen quickly. A digital staff overview makes it easier for supervisors to:

- Identify gaps in coverage
- Reassign people during peak demand
- Document who was responsible for a unit on a given shift

Temetro keeps that information close to the rest of the operational dashboard so teams do not have to jump between unrelated systems.
