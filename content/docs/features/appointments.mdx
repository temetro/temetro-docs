Appointments are one of the clearest places where software can reduce stress for staff and waiting time for patients.

## Core goals

The appointment workspace helps teams:

- Book and review visits quickly
- See clinician ownership
- Track the current state of each visit
- Reduce confusion between reception and clinical units

## Current statuses

Temetro includes appointment states such as:

- Scheduled
- Confirmed
- In progress
- Completed
- Cancelled
- No show

These statuses support a clearer picture of the clinic day and make later reporting easier.

## Typical use cases

- Outpatient clinics with recurring follow-ups
- Specialist review days with heavy morning demand
- Surgical review and discharge appointments
- Private or mission hospitals coordinating multiple units with a small admin team

## What can be extended later

The current data model is a strong base for future additions like:

- SMS reminders
- Doctor availability calendars
- Visit notes
- Queue analytics
