description: Koyi yadda Temetro ke sarrafa booking, layukan bita, da jadawalin marasa lafiya na waje.
---

Alƙawura suna daga cikin wuraren da software zai iya rage damuwar ma'aikata da lokacin jiran marasa lafiya.

## Manufofin asali

Sashen alƙawura yana taimaka wa ƙungiyoyi su:

- tsara da bita ziyara cikin sauri
- ganin wanda likita ko jami'i ne ke da alhakin ziyara
- bin diddigin matsayin ziyara
- rage ruɗani tsakanin reception da sassan asibiti

## Matsayoyi na yanzu

Temetro yana da matsayi kamar:

- An tsara
- An tabbatar
- Ana ci gaba
- An kammala
- An soke
- Mara lafiya bai zo ba

## Amfani na yau da kullum

- asibitocin waje masu bita akai-akai
- ranakun kwararru masu cunkoso da safe
- alƙawuran bayan tiyata da sallama
- asibitoci masu zaman kansu ko mission da ke daidaita sassa da yawa da ƙaramar ƙungiyar admin

## Abin da za a iya faɗaɗa daga baya

Tsarin bayanan yanzu yana ba da tushe ga:

- tunatarwar SMS
- jadawalin samuwar likitoci
- bayanin ziyara
- nazarin layi
